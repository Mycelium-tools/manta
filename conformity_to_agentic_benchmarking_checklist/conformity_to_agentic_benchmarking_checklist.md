# Conformity to the Agentic Benchmarking Checklist

The Agentic Benchmarking Checklist (ABC) was developed by evaluators at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign to guide the development of agentic evaluations, and can be found at https://uiuc-kang-lab.github.io/agentic-benchmarks/

According to them, their broad theory is there exists two main types of validity in evals:

> Task Validity: a task should be solvable if and only if the agent possesses the target capability.
> 
> Outcome Validity: the evaluation method (e.g., tests or checks) should indicate correctly whether the task has been solved.

Their complete checklist is at https://uiuc-kang-lab.github.io/agentic-benchmarks/assets/checklist.pdf to which the shorthand below is correlated.
